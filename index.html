Pong Shooter!

Rules

1. Who ever hit 8 points, win.

2. When any player reach 5 points, a second ball will be added to the game.

3. You can score 1 point by shooting the other players paddle.

Have fun!

Player 1

Controls

Up: W

Down: A

Shoot: D

Player 2

Controls

Up: Arrow Up

Down: Arrow Down

Shoot: Arrow Left
